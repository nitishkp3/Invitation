By Air

There are two close by Airports

1. Patna

* From Patna Airport, go to the nearest Railway Station (Hajipur/Patliputra) to get a train to Siwan.

2. Gorakhpur

*From Gorakhpur Airport, go to the nearest Railway Station(Gorakhpur) to get a train to Siwan.

By Rail

The nearest Railway Station is Siwan.

If there are no direct train to Siwan, then come to Gorakhpur or Chhapra.

There are lot of options available from Gorakhpur and Chhapra to come to Siwan.

**We will pick up from Siwan Railway Station**
